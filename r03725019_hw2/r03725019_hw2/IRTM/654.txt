american businessman edmond pope has been found guilty of espionage 
in a russian court. he's been sentenced to 20 years in prison. pope 
was charged with trying to buy classified information on a russian 
torpedo. he maintains the data had been previously published and sold 
abroad. the 54-year-old has a rare form of bone cancer. pope's wife 
and u.s. officials have been calling for his release. his attorneys 
have seven days to appeal the sentence.
