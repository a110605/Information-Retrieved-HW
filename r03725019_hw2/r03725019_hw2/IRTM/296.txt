a russian court has rejected appeals by an american businessman who 
was arrested on spy charges in april. edmund pope, a businessman and 
former navy intelligence officer, has been diagnosed with a rare form 
of bone cancer. pope's lawyer wanted the court to allow a full medical 
examination that includes his own doctors. russian authorities say 
the prison doctors are adequate, and that pope is well enough to stand 
trial. he is accused of trying to buy secret information on a torpedo.
