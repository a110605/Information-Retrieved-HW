an international team is preparing to try to recover the bodies of 
the 118 crew members of the sunken russian nuclear submarine "kursk." 
the operation is based on a surface ship and includes norwegian and 
russian divers. the sub sank in the barents sea in august. the russians 
are doing preliminary work to create an opening in the hull, backed 
up by the norwegians in a diving bell. the actual cutting is not expected 
to begin before tuesday.
