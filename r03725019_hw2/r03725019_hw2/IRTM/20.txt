in yugoslavia, at least six cities lost power today in an attempt 
by striking coal miners to get president slobodan milosevic to admit 
defeat in recent elections. in the capital belgrade, students and 
opposition party supporters rallied as the government warned of a 
crackdown on so-called "subversives." businesses and factories also 
closed in support of opposition candidate vojislav kostunica, the 
yugoslav election commission claims he did not win more than 50% of 
the vote. a runoff election is set for october
