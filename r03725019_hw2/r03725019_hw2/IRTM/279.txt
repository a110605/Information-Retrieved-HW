the body of missouri governor mel carnahan is lying in state at the 
capitol building in jefferson city today. carnahan, who was running 
for the u.s. senate, was killed along with his son and a top campaign 
aide when their twin-engine plane crashed near st. louis monday night. 
mourners have placed flowers along the fence outside the governor's 
mansion. investigators say carnahan's son, who was piloting the plane, 
reported problems with navigational equipment, and weather data showed 
shear winds in the area at the time of the crash.
