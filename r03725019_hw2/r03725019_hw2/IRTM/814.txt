there are renewed reports former financier michael milken will receive 
a full pardon from president clinton. according to "the new york times," 
the pardon could be announced within days. the "junk bond king" has 
lobbied aggressively for the pardon, citing his record of philanthropy. 
and "the times" reports he had some help in pressing his case from 
a close friend and contributor to mr. clinton. milken served nearly 
two years in prison for securities fraud.
